Primitives
==========
The integer primitives in the language are:

- **byte** (1 byte)
- **short** (2 bytes)
- **int** (4 bytes)
- **long** (8 bytes)

*All integer primitives have an **unsigned** variant by preceding an **u***

----------
The floating precision primitives are:
- **float** (4 bytes)
- **double** (8 bytes)
